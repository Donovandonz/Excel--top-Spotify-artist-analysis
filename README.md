## 📊 Project Overview
An interactive analytics dashboard visualizing Spotify's top 100 songs, featuring comprehensive streaming metrics, artist performance analysis, and genre distribution patterns from **January 2023 to November 2024**.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Pivot Tables for data aggregation
  - Pivot Charts for visualization
  - Slicers for interactive filtering
  - Conditional Formatting for highlighting trends
  - Formulas for calculations (SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, AVERAGE, etc.)

### 📊 Understanding the Metrics
- **Total Streams**: 101.42M = Combined streams of all top 100 songs
- **Streams per Second**: 20,148 = Average streaming rate across all tracks
- **Avg Streams per Song**: 5.03 = Average number of times each song is streamed per second
- **Peak Performance**: Top song reached 1.79M total streams

   - Indie artists competing with major labels

3. **Afrobeats Breakthrough** (13.75M)
   - Remarkable 3rd place finish

4. **Traditional Genres Shift**
   - Pop at #5 (11.69M) - lower than expected
   - Rock at #7 (8.27M) - maintaining core audience
   - Latin at #9 (5.76M) - potential for growth

5. **Genre Diversity**
   - Top 3 genres account for 45% of total streams
   - Remaining 6 genres share 55% - healthy diversity

### Genre-by-Genre Strategic Recommendations 

#### 🥇 #1 HIP-HOP (16.71M)
**Status:** Dominant Leader
**Action:** 🔴 HIGH PRIORITY - Invest Heavily
- 39% year-over-year growth
- 4 of top 5 streaming months in 2024
- Key artists driving massive numbers (Kanye West: 3.78M, Drake: 970K, Travis Scott: 1.69M)
- **Recommendation:** Expand Hip-Hop playlists, prioritize Hip-Hop artist promotions

#### 🥈 #2 INDIE (15.15M)
**Status:** Breakout Success Story
**Action:** 🔴 HIGH PRIORITY - Support Growth
- Jumped from #8 to #2 - biggest mover (+6 positions)
- 15.15M streams without major label backing
- Indie Dragons leading the charge (210K in Nov 2024)
- **Recommendation:** Create dedicated indie discovery platforms, support indie artist development

#### 🥉 #3 AFROBEATS (13.75M)
**Status:** Fastest Rising Genre
**Action:** 🔴 HIGH PRIORITY - Capitalize Now
- 180% growth rate - highest in dataset
- 5.43 streams/sec efficiency (2nd highest)
- Summer surge shows 3x normal streams
- **Recommendation:** Aggressive investment, summer festival headlining slots

#### 4️⃣ #4 ELECTRONIC (13.44M)
**Status:** Declining Former Leader
**Action:** 🟡 MEDIUM PRIORITY - Revitalize
- 45% drop from #1 in 2023 to #4 in 2024
- Still maintains strong 5.21 streams/sec
- Festival-dependent pattern
- **Recommendation:** Diversify beyond festivals, create year-round engagement

#### 5️⃣ #5 POP (11.69M)
**Status:** Traditional Power Slips
**Action:** 🟢 LOW PRIORITY - Maintain/Refresh
- Lowest ranking in modern history
- Stable but not growing (4.87 streams/sec)
- Key artists still deliver (Taylor Swift, Olivia Rodrigo, Justin Bieber)
- **Recommendation:** Seek fresh talent, innovate sound, leverage holiday Q4
